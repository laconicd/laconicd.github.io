My First Zola Site

A
Author Name
Writer

Building my first static site with Zola has been an amazing experience. The speed and simplicity of writing content in Markdown, combined with Zola's powerful templating engine, makes it a joy to work with.

This post will delve into some of the key features I found most useful, such as:

  • Markdown-first approach: How easy it is to focus on content.
  • Tera templating: Customizing the look and feel.
  • Built-in features: RSS feeds, sitemaps, and more.

Stay tuned for more updates on my Zola journey!
